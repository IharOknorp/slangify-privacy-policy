## 2. What the App Does

Slangify allows users to enter text and generate stylized or slang-based rewritten output. The App also includes advertising, rewarded ads, analytics, support purchases, and language/settings preferences.

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

- Text you enter for translation or rewriting
- Selected style or "vibe"
- Generated output returned to you

### B. App preferences and local data

- Selected app language
- Local settings
- Locally stored recent translation result
- Local generation counters and bonus generation state

### C. Authentication and backend identifiers

- Anonymous Firebase Authentication user ID

Through Firebase Analytics and related Google services, the App or our providers may process:

- App instance identifiers
- Device and app information, such as model, operating system, app version, and language
- Usage events, sessions, and app interaction data

- Diagnostic, anti-fraud, and performance data

### E. Advertising data

Through Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads, the App or our providers may process:

- Advertising ID or similar device identifiers, where available
- Ad request and response data
- Ad impressions, clicks, rewarded ad events, and related interaction data
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- Device information
- Consent and privacy-choice status

### F. Purchase and entitlement data

If you make a support purchase, our billing and purchase infrastructure may process:

- Purchase status
- Product identifiers
- Entitlement status
- Transaction metadata necessary to validate and restore purchases

We do not receive your full payment card details.
